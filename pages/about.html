About me

Hi! I’m Farrhan, though many know me as Dip. I’m a video editor, and designer based in Kuala Lumpur. My creative journey started all in 2014 with nothing more than a few funny voice call recordings and a YouTube channel (All of my old videos are deleted, but you can try your best to find them). Today, that hobby has evolved into a professional role as a video editor.

I tend to treat video editing as a balance between corporate polish and the high-energy chaos of "shitposting". To keep my sanity in check and avoid burnout, I collaborate with VTubers and live streamers to experiment with different editing styles that- hopefully- stands out from the typical render.

When I’m not scrubbing through a timeline, I’m usually playing bass, shooting film, tinkering with tech, or getting lost in a JRPG. I’ve been a creator for over a decade, and I don’t plan on stopping anytime soon.
