About

Farrhan, otherwise known as "Dip", is a video editor (and sometimes graphic designer) based in Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia, currently finishing his studies while working at Grass Konbini, a media and marketing company.

He has been editing videos since 2014- long before it became a career- turning a childhood obsession into a professional craft. Since 2021, he has brought that same instinct to client work, producing content that is deliberate, considered, and made to last.

Outside of the timeline, Dip is a bassist, an audiophile quietly losing the battle against gear acquisition syndrome, and a committed JRPG enjoyer. He is working toward building and leading a media team of his own.
